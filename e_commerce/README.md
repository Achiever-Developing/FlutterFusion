# e_commerce

- Welcome to our E-Commerce App! This README file provides an overview of the features and functionalities of our application.

## Features:

### Add to Cart:
- Users can add products to their shopping cart with a single click.

### Remove from Cart:
- Users can easily remove products from their shopping cart if they change their mind.

### Add to Favorites:
- Users can mark products as favorites for quick access and future reference.

### Remove from Favorites:
- Users can remove products from their favorites list if they no longer wish to keep them.

### Navigation:
- The app features intuitive navigation, allowing users to seamlessly browse through different categories, products, and sections.

### Counter (+/-):
- Users can adjust the quantity of products in their cart using convenient plus and minus buttons.

### Light and Dark Mode:
- The app supports both light and dark mode, providing users with a personalized viewing experience.
